High Performance Graphics Card

Our most recent iteration of graphics cards features fifth-generation RDNA cores.
The specifications are as follows:

Specifications:

This card features 20GB of DDR6 Vram, as well as our patented fifth-generation Ampere type RDNA cores.

Dimensions:

  • Length: 11.5in
  • Width: 3.7in

Installation:

Remove the graphics card from its packaged casing. Take extra caution not to damage any pins on the underside.

Remove the previous graphics card by unscrewing case holders and removing it from the motherboard.

Install the new graphics card, insert it into the pin holders on the motherboard, and screw in the new case holders.
